who are we?

MYTHOS is a publishing collective. we give an outlet for creatives of any notoriety to gain an audience for their work.
we publish short-storys, essays, reviews, thoughts - any creative work has the chance of being published.
we are currently a not-for-profit organization with the sole focus of giving artists an outlet to express their creativity.
if you are interested in submitting your work, please visit our submissions page

if you have any questions or concerns, please email us at contact@mythospublishing.me
